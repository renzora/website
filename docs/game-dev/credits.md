# Credits System

Renzora Credits are the virtual currency used to buy and sell assets on the Marketplace.

## What are credits?

Credits are Renzora's digital currency. Use them to purchase assets, and earn them by selling your own creations.

**1 credit ≈ $0.01 USD**

## Purchasing credits

Buy credits from your [Wallet](/wallet) page.

| Package | Credits | Price | Bonus |
|---------|---------|-------|-------|
| Starter | 500 | $5 | — |
| Standard | 1,000 | $10 | — |
| Pro | 2,500 | $25 | — |
| Studio | 5,000 | $45 | 10% bonus |
| Enterprise | 10,000 | $85 | 15% bonus |

Payment methods: credit card, debit card, PayPal.

## Using credits

1. Find an asset on the [Marketplace](/marketplace)
2. Click **Buy for X credits**
3. Credits are deducted from your wallet
4. The asset is added to your Library

Free assets cost 0 credits — just click **Get**.

## Earning credits as a creator

When someone buys your asset:

- **You receive 70%** of the purchase price
- **Renzora takes 30%** (covers hosting, bandwidth, payment processing)

Example: you price an asset at 500 credits. Each sale earns you 350 credits.

## Payouts

Convert earned credits to real money:

1. Go to [Wallet](/wallet) → **Payout**
2. Minimum payout: 5,000 credits ($50)
3. Payout methods: PayPal, bank transfer
4. Payouts are processed within 7 business days
5. Payouts are made monthly (if minimum is reached)

## Transaction history

View all transactions in [Wallet](/wallet) → **History**:

- Credits purchased
- Assets bought (credits spent)
- Sales received (credits earned)
- Payouts processed

Each transaction shows date, amount, and description.

## Promo codes

Redeem promo codes for free credits:

1. Go to [Wallet](/wallet)
2. Click **Redeem Code**
3. Enter the code

Promo codes are distributed through:

- Renzora events and game jams
- Community giveaways
- Partnership promotions

## Refund policy

- **Within 24 hours** of purchase, you can request a full refund if you haven't downloaded the asset
- **After download**, refunds are reviewed case-by-case
- **Not eligible**: assets that have been integrated into a shipped project
- Request refunds via your transaction history

## Gifting

Send credits to another user:

1. Go to [Wallet](/wallet) → **Send Credits**
2. Enter the recipient's username
3. Enter the amount
4. Confirm the transfer

Transfers are instant and non-reversible.
